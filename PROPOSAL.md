## My Project Proposal

**What I'm building:** I am building a text analyzing tool to compare three decade-defining songs from the 1980's: You Make My Dreams Come True by Hall and Oates, Don't Dream It's Over by Crowded House, and Everybody Wants To Rule The World by Tears for Fears.

**Why I chose this:** These are some of my favorite songs from the 1980's and I am curious to see how they compare in terms of vocabularies, word count frequencies, and other text based statistics. 

**Core features:** 
- read three txt files of each song
- cleaning each file to adjust based on punctuation and capitalization
- give total word count and unique word count
- find most frequently used words
- use average word length to determine reading level
- find shared vocabulary between all three songs
- filter out common words such as conjunctions, articles, and propositions
